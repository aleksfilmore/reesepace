About Best Efforts

A market grant puts Lia’s community‑arts studio under a spotlight she never wanted — and into forced proximity with Theo, the PR‑minded analyst who tagged her work a “PR asset.” Integrity clashes with optics, banter sparks into heat, and a public choice forces both to show their best efforts.

About the Author

Reese Pace believes the best love stories happen in the spaces between—between market stalls and late-night planning sessions, between first impressions and deeper truths. A Chicago native who's spent countless hours in community centers, coffee shops, and farmers markets, Reese captures the electric moment when two people realize they're exactly where they need to be. Each Union Arc romance is designed to be devoured in one sitting, because sometimes you need a complete escape and a guaranteed happy ending in 90 minutes flat.
