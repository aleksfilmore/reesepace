About Best Efforts

Ceramicist Lia Santos must deliver a safe, crowd-friendly art pilot or see Union Arc Market shut for 30 days and lose half its funding. Grant writer Theo Kline brings the keys, the timeline, and a clause that keeps them on-site 24/7.

Sawdust becomes sparks. A careful boundary becomes one unforgettable night. Then a city press release erases Lia's credit and lights up the internet. The maze, the money, and their fragile trust are all on the line.

He can fix logistics. She owns the story. Together, they might save both.

Read it if you love:

  • Forced proximity + six-day countdown
  • Competence, consent, and open-door spice
  • Grumpy/capable x grumpy/capable
  • Found family at a fictional Chicago market
  • Real stakes: permits, press, donors, safety drills
  • A clean HEA with no cliffhanger

About the Author

Reese Pace believes the best love stories happen in the spaces between‚Äîbetween market stalls and late-night planning sessions, between first impressions and deeper truths. A Chicago native who's spent countless hours in community centers, coffee shops, and farmers markets, Reese captures the electric moment when two people realize they're exactly where they need to be. Each Union Arc romance is designed to be devoured in one sitting, because sometimes you need a complete escape and a guaranteed happy ending in 90 minutes flat.
